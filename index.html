Duelling Mythicals

Cards in the game

What is it?

Duelling Mythicals is a fantasy-themed, player vs. computer digital card game. Its core mechanic resembles 'rock paper scissors'.

How do I play it?

  • You and the computer will start with 25-card decks, containing 5 of each mythical creature.
  • On each go, you will have a free choice of which creature to duel with, so long as there is at least 1 of it remaining in your deck.
  • The computer will play a card randomly out of its remaining cards.
  • Your individual wins, losses, and draws will be remembered while the game is in progress. An overall victory, stalemate, or defeat will be calculated from them.
